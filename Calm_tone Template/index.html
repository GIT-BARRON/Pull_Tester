About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Technology. My research interests include machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

Prior to joining the University of Technology, I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University and worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at MIT.
